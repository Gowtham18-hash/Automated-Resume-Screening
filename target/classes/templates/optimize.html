Keywords are Key

Tailor your resume to the job description. Use exact keywords found in the job post, as ATS algorithms scan for these matches.

Simple Formatting

Avoid complex layouts, columns, and graphics. Use standard section headings like "Experience", "Education", and "Skills".

Quantify Achievements

Use numbers to demonstrate your impact. E.g., "Increased sales by 20%" or "Reduced load time by 3s".

Relevant Skills

List technical and soft skills separately. Ensure your top skills match the requirements of the role you're applying for.

Proofread

Spelling and grammar mistakes can be a red flag. Double-check your resume or use tools like Grammarly.
